At DevByHans Wishes, your privacy is our top priority. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We **do not** collect, store, or misuse any personal data you enter while creating wishes. All wish generation happens directly in your browser.
  • We **do not** use cookies for tracking purposes or to store your personal information.
  • We **do not** collect IP addresses, browser information, or device details.

Information We May Collect (Anonymously)

  • **Usage Data:** We may use simple, privacy-focused analytics (e.g., counting page visits) to understand general traffic patterns, but this data is fully anonymized and cannot be linked to individual users. This helps us improve our service.

Third-Party Services

  • **FormSubmit.co:** Our contact form uses FormSubmit.co to send your messages directly to our email. We do not store your contact details on our server. Please refer to FormSubmit.co's privacy policy for their data handling practices.
  • **Telegram:** If you choose to join our Telegram group, your interaction is governed by Telegram's privacy policy.

Data Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. While we do not collect personal data for wishes, we implement reasonable security measures to protect the integrity of our website.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
