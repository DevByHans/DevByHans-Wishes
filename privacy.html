Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information that are processed by DevByHans Wishes and how we handle it. We are committed to safeguarding your personal information and ensuring a transparent approach to data handling.

Our website primarily functions as a client-side wish generator. This means that any input you provide, such as names or custom messages, is processed directly within your web browser to generate the wish card. **We do not store, collect, or transmit any of this personalized input data on our servers.** Your wishes are generated and exist ephemerally within your device until you choose to share them, ensuring your privacy remains intact.

We do not use cookies for tracking personal identifiable information. We may, however, use basic, non-intrusive session cookies solely for essential website functionality (e.g., maintaining your preference for the duration of your visit). These are automatically deleted once you close your browser. We also do not employ web beacons, pixels, or similar technologies to gather personal data.

For analytics purposes, we may utilize **Google Analytics** to understand website traffic and usage patterns (e.g., which pages are most popular, how long visitors stay). This data is anonymized and aggregated, meaning it cannot be linked back to individual users. It helps us improve our website's performance and content without compromising your personal identity. Google Analytics' use of data is governed by the Google Analytics Terms of Service and the Google Privacy Policy.

Our Contact page utilizes a third-party service, **FormSubmit**, to facilitate form submissions. When you send a message through the Contact form, your name, email, and message are transmitted directly to us via this secure third-party service. We only use this information to respond to your inquiry and do not store it beyond the necessity of correspondence. FormSubmit has its own privacy policy, which we encourage you to review.

We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of any other site that collects Personally Identifiable Information.

If you think that your child provided this kind of information on our website, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately and we will do our best efforts to promptly remove such information from our records.
